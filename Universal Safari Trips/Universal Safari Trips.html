About Us

The Universal Safari Trip, together with RL Travel and Tours, is a travel company duly registered in Uganda, Kenya, Tanzania, South Africa, Congo, and the Philippines. We provide and promote travel as well as tourism-related services to the general public and work with our customer suppliers and partners to offer different traveling packages for each destination for both local and international tourists.

Mission

To help promote tourism locally and internationally, providing a competitive and reliable source of tourism that our guests, both local and international, will enjoy during their travel experiences. We aim to give a total experience to our beloved guests as a travel and tours company.

Vision

As a travel agency, we provide sustainable and attainable tour packages to our guests, both local and international. We ensure their security and strive to give our guests a great and memorable experience during their tours.
