About Us

Can you ceramic coat your wrapped vehicles? Absolutely!

We offer a ceramic coating service called WRAP-Guard that is applied in 2 layers to protect your wrapped vehicle.

  • WRAP-Guard is a flexible nano-coating product that increases the performance of paint protection films and vinyl wraps.
  • It protects the applied surface against various factors and extends the life of the surface.
  • This service provides hydrophobic and UV resistance, is easy to clean, and won't spoil the physical properties of glossy, matte, or satin surfaces.
  • It also prevents color fading caused by sun rays and does not cause any changes in the structure of the film.
  • 100% Customer Satisfaction Guaranteed
  • Licensed and Insured
  • Use of eco-friendly products
